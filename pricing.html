FAQ

Pricing Questions

Do you charge for the initial visit? +
No. Our plumber will assess the problem and give you a free quote. You only pay if you approve the work.
Are there extra charges for evenings or weekends? +
Standard rates apply Monday–Saturday. Sunday visits carry a small surcharge of ₹150. Emergency (24/7) has a flat call-out fee of ₹799 regardless of time.
What payment methods do you accept? +
We accept UPI (PhonePe, Google Pay, Paytm), cash, and all major debit/credit cards. GST invoice available on request.
Do you offer a guarantee on your work? +
Yes. All labour is guaranteed for 30 days. If the same issue recurs within that period, we return and fix it at no charge. Parts carry the manufacturer's warranty.
